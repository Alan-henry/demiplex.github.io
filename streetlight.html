A.I based automatic Street Lights

Here at Demiplex we use an A.I controlled street light system which is solar powered but has an electrical power supply just in case, our A.i can automatically detect the time of the day thus freeing us from going through the manual labour of turning the lights on which might in some cases be inconsistent and unreliable. Since we use solar power as an initial approach we can reduce the utilisation of taxes in this branch by a significant amount and utilise them elsewhere. All the streets come equipped with security cameras so that all of the residents feel safe while walking around even when they are alone.
